SWF Background Color

Function: Changes the background colour of the SWF application.

Parameter: hexidecimal color code (RRGGBB) e.g. ffffff = white, 000000 = black,

Quick colour list:

Bright: red=ff0000, green=00ff00, blue=0000ff, yellow=ffff00, cyan=00ffff, fuchia=ff00ff, orange=ff6600

Dark: red=990000, green=009900, blue=000099, yellow=ff9900, cyan=0099ff, fuchia=9900ff, brown=663300

Default: none

Please note: Take care when changing the background color, as text and graphics may become difficult to see or even invisible. e.g. black text on a black background!
